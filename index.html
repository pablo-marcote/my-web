Why following this team?

The UD Almeria football club was born in 2001 after the merging of the two main local teams pursuing bigger challenges: Polideportivo Almeria and Almeria CF.

Just 5 years after its creation, the club managed to promote to the Spanish 1st Division on the 2006/2007 season, finishing on the 8th position that same year.

On 2 August 2019, Turki Al-Sheikh became the owner of the club, replacing the former president, promising big investments in the city and in the club.

Nowadays, the club is runnning 1st on Spanish 2nd Division with half of the season still to play. The promotion is closer week after week!
